Features

Servo Motor Automatic Door

Automate your door movements with a reliable servo motor mechanism. This system allows the door to open 45 degrees forward on the press of a button and move backward when pressed again.

Magnet Door Alarm

The magnet door alarm uses a microbit to detect magnetic force changes when a door is opened, triggering a loud, continuous alarm.
